Sausage Potato Casserole

Description

This sausage potato casserole is a great family friendly mid-week meal that requires little preparation.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large russet potatoes
  • 1 poound bulk milk Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped red sweet pepper
  • 1 can cpndensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 cup shredded pepper Jack Cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black paper
  • finely chopped chives

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 2-quart baking dish.
  3. Cook sliced potatoes in a large saucepan of lightly salted, boiling water for 5 minutes. Drain.
  4. Meanwhile, cook sausage, in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat until no longer pink, breaking it into crumbles. Add onion and peppers; cook about 3 minutes more or until just beginning to soften; drain sausage mixture and set aside.
  5. Whisk together soup and milk in a medium bowl. Stir in 3/4 cup of the cheese and black pepper.
  6. Layer half the potatoes, soup mixture, and sausage mixture in the prepared dish. Repeat layers.
  7. Cover and bake until potatoes are tender, 45 to 50 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining 1 1/4 cups cheese. Bake, uncovered, until cheese is melted, 10 minutes.
  8. Let stand 15 minutes before serving. Garnish with chives.
